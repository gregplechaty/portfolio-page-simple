Too Many Pieces?

When the quantity of tools is immense, and your skills are not, how do you know where to start?

January 15th, 2021
Back

Part of what makes coding terrifying is the interconnectness of coding languages. Python, by itself, is taught with small logic puzzles. I supposed this is why html is a common starting point: not only is it extremely simple, but you can create a' mvp' (minimally viable product) by itself. Add a little CSS, and you have a website that looks like it was plucked from then 90s.
